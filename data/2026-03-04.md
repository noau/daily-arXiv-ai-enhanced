### [1] [Generalized non-exponential Gaussian splatting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02887)
*Sébastien Speierer,Adrian Jarabo*

Main category: cs.GR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于物理的alpha-blending操作的3D高斯泼溅（3DGS）的通用化方法，扩展了其在非指数辐射传输中的应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 3DGS因其灵活性和高效性已成为辐射场渲染和重建的标准方法，但现有的3DGS基于指数传输模型。受非指数辐射传输研究的启发，本文旨在将其扩展至非指数领域。

Method: 通过将图像形成模型推广到非指数领域，并使用二次透射率定义了亚线性、线性和超线性版本的3DGS，这些版本表现出比指数更快或更慢的衰减特性。

Result: 实验表明，这些非指数变体在保持与原始3DGS相似质量的同时，显著减少了过绘次数，在复杂真实场景中速度提升高达4倍。

Conclusion: 研究表明，非指数版本的3DGS能够高效地应用于辐射场渲染，并在性能上取得显著提升。

Abstract: In this work we generalize 3D Gaussian splatting (3DGS) to a wider family of physically-based alpha-blending operators. 3DGS has become the standard de-facto for radiance field rendering and reconstruction, given its flexibility and efficiency. At its core, it is based on alpha-blending sorted semitransparent primitives, which in the limit converges to the classic radiative transfer function with exponential transmittance. Inspired by recent research on non-exponential radiative transfer, we generalize the image formation model of 3DGS to non-exponential regimes. Based on this generalization, we use a quadratic transmittance to define sub-linear, linear, and super-linear versions of 3DGS, which exhibit faster-than-exponential decay. We demonstrate that these new non-exponential variants achieve similar quality than the original 3DGS but significantly reduce the number of overdraws, which result on speed-ups of up to $4\times$ in complex real-world captures, on a ray-tracing-based renderer.

*Ethan Chu,Yiyang Guo,Jan Hoffmann*

Main category: cs.PL

TL;DR: 本文提出了第一个支持异常或效果处理程序中非局部控制转移的自动资源边界分析方法，扩展了基于类型的自动摊销资源分析（AARA）。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管现有技术能有效分析程序源代码的资源边界，但异常或效果处理所需的非局部控制转移仍是一个挑战。本文旨在解决这一问题。

Method: 该方法扩展了基于类型的自动摊销资源分析（AARA），支持非局部控制转移，并与堆栈式抽象机的正确性建立了新颖的语法类型安全定理。

Result: 实验评估表明，新方法能分析现有技术无法处理的程序，且支持异常和效果处理程序的效率开销较低。

Conclusion: 本文为异常和效果处理程序的自动资源边界分析提供了首个解决方案，展示了其在丰富语言环境中的直接适用性和高效性。

Abstract: There exist many techniques for automatically deriving parametric resource (or cost) bounds by analyzing the source code of a program. These techniques work effectively for a large class of programs and language features. However, non-local transfer of control as needed for exception or effect handlers has remained a challenge.
  This paper presents the first automatic resource bound analysis that supports non-local control transfer between exceptions or effects and their handlers. The analysis is an extension of type-based automatic amortized resource analysis (AARA), which automates the potential method of amortized analysis. It is presented for a simple functional language with lists and linear potential functions. However, the ideas are directly applicable to richer settings and implemented for Standard ML and polynomial potential functions.
  Apart from the new type system for exceptions and effects, a main contribution is a novel syntactic type-soundness theorem that establishes the correctness of the derived bounds with respect to a stack-based abstract machine. An experimental evaluation shows that the new analysis is capable of analyzing programs that cannot be analyzed by existing methods and that the efficiency overhead of supporting exception and effect handlers is low.

### [6] [Delegation and Verification Under AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02961)
*Lingxiao Huang,Wenyang Xiao,Nisheeth K. Vishnoi*

Main category: cs.GT

TL;DR: 论文研究了AI系统进入机构工作流程后，工人如何决定任务委派给AI以及验证AI输出的努力程度，揭示了AI如何通过结构性机制改变工人的质量和加剧质量差异。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机在于探讨AI系统进入机构工作流程时，工人和机构之间的目标不一致如何影响任务委派和验证行为，以及AI如何放大工人之间的质量差异。

Method: 研究通过建模工人优化问题来分析任务委派和验证行为，定义了以机构为中心的效用函数来评估工人质量，并形式化描述了最优工作流程。

Result: 结果表明，AI可能导致行为突变，微小验证能力的差异引发显著不同的行为；AI可能强化验证能力强的工人，同时降低其他人的机构工人质量，即使任务成功率提高且没有行为偏差。

We formally characterize optimal worker workflows and show that AI induces *phase transitions*, where arbitrarily small differences in verification ability lead to sharply different behaviors. As a result, AI can amplify workers with strong verification reliability while degrading institutional worker quality for others who rationally over-delegate and reduce oversight, even when baseline task success improves and no behavioral biases are present. These results identify a structural mechanism by which AI reshapes institutional worker quality and amplifies quality disparities between workers with different verification reliability.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探讨在中介参与的三方双边交易中,利用有限的单一样本数据设计高效交易机制的可能性,为在线市场和去中心化交易平台提供数据高效的中介机制设计思路。
