### [1] [Robust and Efficient Penetration-Free Elastodynamics without Barriers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12151)
*Juntian Zheng,Zhaofeng Luo,Minchen Li*

Main category: cs.GR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种无障礙优化的非穿透弹性动力学模拟框架，克服了IPC方法的两个主要效率瓶颈，包括对数障礙函数导致的系统病态和时间锁定问题，并通过新型二阶约束优化框架和自适应拉格朗日乘子更新实现了显著的性能提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的增量势能接触(IPC)方法在处理非穿透约束时存在效率瓶颈问题，包括对数障礙函数导致的系统病态和时间锁定问题，限制了其在碰撞密集型场景中的应用。

Method: 提出了一种新型二阶约束优化框架，采用自定义增广拉格朗日求解器，避免了时间锁定问题。同时引入自适应拉格朗日乘子更新和约束过滤衰减机制，保持系统良好状态和活跃集的紧凑性。

Result: 实验结果表明，该方法在接触密集的基准测试中比GIPC方法提升了高达103倍的速度，并且在效率和准确性上表现优异。

Conclusion: 该方法通过克服IPC方法的效率瓶颈，提供了一种高效、稳健且准确的弹性动力学模拟解决方案，未来将开源代码和数据。

Abstract: We introduce a barrier-free optimization framework for non-penetration elastodynamic simulation that matches the robustness of Incremental Potential Contact (IPC) while overcoming its two primary efficiency bottlenecks: (1) reliance on logarithmic barrier functions to enforce non-penetration constraints, which leads to ill-conditioned systems and significantly slows down the convergence of iterative linear solvers; and (2) the time-of-impact (TOI) locking issue, which restricts active-set exploration in collision-intensive scenes and requires a large number of Newton iterations. We propose a novel second-order constrained optimization framework featuring a custom augmented Lagrangian solver that avoids TOI locking by immediately incorporating all requisite contact pairs detected via CCD, enabling more efficient active-set exploration and leading to significantly fewer Newton iterations. By adaptively updating Lagrange multipliers rather than increasing penalty stiffness, our method prevents stagnation at zero TOI while maintaining a well-conditioned system. We further introduce a constraint filtering and decay mechanism to keep the active set compact and stable, along with a theoretical justification of our method's finite-step termination and first-order time integration accuracy under a cumulative TOI-based termination criterion. A comprehensive set of experiments demonstrates the efficiency, robustness, and accuracy of our method. With a GPU-optimized simulator design, our method achieves an up to 103x speedup over GIPC on challenging, contact-rich benchmarks - scenarios that were previously tractable only with barrier-based methods. Our code and data will be open-sourced.

### [2] [Differentially Private Online Distributed Aggregative Games With Time-Varying and Non-Identical Communication and Feedback Delays](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12344)
*Olusola Odeyomi,Tokunbo Ogunfunmi,Adjovi Laba*

Main category: cs.GT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了具有时变成本函数的在线分布式聚合博弈，解决了隐私泄露和时变非一致通信与反馈延迟的挑战，并提出了一种在线分布式双平均算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 由于分布式聚合博弈的非合作性，某些代理商可能试图窃取邻近代理商的敏感信息。此外，网络拥塞和反馈延迟会影响代理商收敛到纳什均衡。现有研究假设延迟固定且一致，而本文首次研究了时变非一致延迟和隐私泄露的联合问题。

Method: 提出了一种在线分布式双平均算法，能够同时应对隐私泄露和时变非一致的通信与反馈延迟，并提供了可证明的低遗憾界限。

Result: 仿真结果表明，每个客户端的本地动作的运行平均值随时间逐渐收敛。

Conclusion: 本文首次解决了聚合博弈中的隐私泄露与时变非一致延迟问题，提出的算法在保证低遗憾的同时实现了收敛。

Abstract: This paper investigates online distributed aggregative games with time-varying cost functions, where agents are interconnected through an unbalanced communication graph. Due to the distributed and noncooperative nature of the game, some curious agents may wish to steal sensitive information from neighboring agents during parameter exchanges. Additionally, communication delays arising from network congestion, particularly in wireless settings, as well as feedback delays, can hinder the convergence of agents to a Nash equilibrium. Although a recent work addressed both communication and feedback delays in aggregative games, it is based on the unrealistic assumption that the delays are fixed over time and identical across agents. Hence, the case of time-varying and non-identical delays across agents has never been considered in aggregative games. In this work, we address the combined challenges of privacy leakage with time-varying and non-identical communication and feedback delays for the first time. We propose an online distributed dual averaging algorithm that simultaneously tackles these challenges while achieving a provably low regret bound. Our simulation result shows that the running average of each client's local action converges over time.

### [4] [Generative AI as Digital Representatives in Collective Decision-Making: A Game-Theoretical Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12582)

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在协作任务中，GenAI作为数字代表为团队成员做出决策时，面临如何准确代表成员偏好的挑战。完全访问成员的个人信息不现实，因此需要一种方法来解决这种有限信息的问题。

Method: 研究采用博弈论框架，分析成员在集体决策中战略性地向GenAI披露信息的均衡行为，并量化数字代表在偏好学习中的不完美结果。

Result: 研究发现，团队成员的偏好冲突会驱动竞争性信息揭示，导致提供的信息比偏好一致的成员更多。此外，尽管数字代表的偏好学习不完美，但在高参与成本或GenAI系统先进时，个体成员可能获得更符合其偏好的决策。

The technical challenges lie in characterizing members' equilibrium behaviors under interdependent strategies and quantifying the imperfect preference learning outcomes by digital representatives. Our contribution includes closed-form equilibrium characterizations that reveal how members strategically balance team decision preference against communication costs. Our analysis yields an interesting finding: Conflicting preferences between team members drive competitive information revelation, with members revealing more information than those with aligned preferences. <details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统调度问题主要关注完工时间最小化，而忽视了代理的战略行为对公平性的影响。本研究旨在结合公平性和战略行为，提出更具实际意义的调度方案。

Result: 论文展示了这些属性单独或组合的可满足性和决策版本的完整复杂性图谱，并在完工时间优化的结构约束下进行了研究。

Conclusion: 研究提出了一种结合公平性和战略行为的调度方法，并通过统一的算法框架实现了多样化的公平性要求，为调度问题提供了新的研究方向。

We present a complete complexity landscape for satisfiability and decision versions of these properties, alone or in combination, and study them as structural constraints under makespan optimization.
